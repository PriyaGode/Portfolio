~ About Me ~

As the daughter of an industrial designer, it didn’t take me long to fall in love with design and technology. For as long as I can remember, I’ve been behind screens, tearing apart designs and websites to discover the craft behind them. This quickly led to working as a freelance web designer and developer alongside school, and it evolved into a true passion. Fast forward a few years, I stumbled across Webflow— I was immediately drawn in and understood its potential, so I went all in. Over the past Eight years, I have worked as a full-time independent Webflow developer, collaborating with clients I once dreamed of. I value honesty and transparency, which guide my work ethic, and I am driven by a desire to create remarkable solutions alongside teams that share my dedication.

With expertise in full-stack development, cloud technologies, and Salesforce CRM, I have a strong command of Java, JavaScript, Angular, and ReactJS, along with hands-on experience in Spring Boot, Hibernate, and AWS. I have a proven track record of developing scalable applications, optimizing workflows, and implementing process improvements that enhance system performance. My Master’s degree in Computer Science from Oklahoma City University has helped solidify my technical foundation.

My work is driven by a commitment to continuous learning and professional growth, with a relentless focus on delivering high-quality software that meets client needs. Whether developing cloud-based applications or refining user experiences, I am passionate about pushing boundaries and achieving excellence. I look forward to collaborating with companies and teams that value innovation, transparency, and a shared dedication to outstanding results.
